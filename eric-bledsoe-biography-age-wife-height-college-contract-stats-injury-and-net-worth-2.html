Who is Eric Bledsoe? Eric Bledsoe Biography- Wiki

Eric Bledsoe is an American professional basketball player who was born and raised in Birmingham, Alabama, United States. Currently, he plays the point guard position for the Milwaukee Bucks of the National Basketball Association. Eric is also able to play the shooting guard position as well.

At first, he was selected by the Oklahoma City Thunder with the 18th overall pick in the 2010 NBA draft. From there, he was subsequently traded to the Los Angeles Clippers. Bledsoe had a 4-year tenure with the Phoenix Suns between 2013 and 2017, before being traded to the Bucks.
