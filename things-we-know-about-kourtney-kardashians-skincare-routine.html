10 Things We Know About Kourtney Kardashian's Skincare Routine

 Posted on June 5, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1126 words  |  Larita Shotwell

As the eldest sister of one of the wealthiest and most famous families in Hollywood, it is no surprise Kourtney Kardashian has a quality skincare routine. After juggling daily life as an A-list celebrity, mom-of-three and owner of Poosh, a lifestyle brand, who wouldn't want to unwind at the end of the day with a relaxing skincare routine? With looking fantastic at forty-two years-old and having access to some of the best skincare products on the market, Kourtney's skincare tips are to be trusted.

The reality television star takes pride in caring for her skin by using a number of clean skincare products and is not afraid of POOSHing her routine to its limits. Although lengthy, her skincare routine does give viewers great information on how to care for their skin in some of the best ways possible. Below are the steps and products Kourtney Kardashian does and uses as part of her nighttime skincare routine.

10 She Begins By Protecting Her Hair

In an episode of Go to Bed with Me, titled Kourtney Kardashian's Nighttime Skincare Routine by Harper's BAZAAR, Kourtney says she always begins her skincare routine by first protecting her hair. She shows in the episode, she uses a Aquis X Poosh: Rapid Dry Leopard Print Hair Turban, she uses it "as a headband" and to wrap her hair to keep her hairstyle for the next day. Kourtney further states, the leopard side of the hair turban is made from silk, which "protects" her hair.

9 She Removes Her Makeup

A crucial step in any skincare routine is the removal of makeup and Kourtney reveals the importance of it in her routine. She shows that she takes off her makeup with a cotton pad and Garnier: Micellar Cleansing Water. She tells Harper's BAZAAR, "I think there's a lot of different brands out there, so i've tried a bunch and they're all the same" and giggles while using the product. The star does praise the feeling of removing her makeup after a long day, saying "it's so nice" and is also necessary in order to do the next step in her routine.

8 She Cleanses Her Skin

After she removes her makeup with the micellar water, Kourtney further cleanses her skin with a face wash. She uses the Peach & Lily: Hydrating Gel Cleanser to wash her face and

The cleanser was apart of a "Poosh Pick of The Week" and she says she loves the facial cleanser because "it doesn't strip your skin" and "makes my skin feel really clean," which is key for her combination type skin.

7 She Applies A Face Mask

When she has thoroughly removed her makeup and cleansed her skin, Kourtney loves to follow up with a face mask. In her routine, she states if she has the time she will  even "alternate three different face masks in one night." The episode shows she uses the Caudalie: Instant Detox Mask and she says this face mask is one of her "favourites." She says in the video, "I just alternate masks depending on what my skin needs" while showing viewers a helpful tip on applying face products in an "upwards" motion, a trick her sister Khloé taught her.

6 She Takes Collagen

While her face mask sets, Kourtney makes it known, she's not going to just watch the face mask dry, she uses this time wisely. In the meantime, she drinks her Vital Proteins X Poosh: Pink Moon Milk Collagen Latte. She explains to Harper's BAZAAR the supplement beverage has "collagen" and shares her belief on "beauty from the inside out." She further explains the health benefits of the drink in the episode and says the latte helps her sleep as "it has melatonin" and also has ashwagandha, "which is an adaptogen, so it adapts to what your body needs." 

5 She Exfoliates Her Lips

Kourtney Kardashian is sure to not waste anytime during her skincare routine and demonstrates how she can fit multiple steps in at the same time. When her face mask is drying and while she consumes her collagen latte, she as well exfoliates her lips. Kourtney uses the French Girl Organics: Rose Lip Polish and mentions, she received the lip scrub from a friend for her birthday. By the time she is done using the exfoliator, her face mask is usually dry and this would be the time she would rinse the face mask off.

4 She Applies Lip Balm

Once the creator of Poosh has exfoliated her lips, she applies a lip balm. Kourtney shows in the episode that she uses the Dr. Barbara Sturm: Lip Balm and raves about the product,

3 She Applies A Facial Serum

The lengthy skincare routine continues with Kourtney applying a facial serum onto her freshly cleansed skin. The face serum she uses in the episode is the Hora X Poosh: Hyaluronic Acid + CBD, which has "Hyaluronic acid, Vitamin C and CBD" she says. She tells Harper's BAZAAR, she uses the serum "nightly" and gives a demonstration on how she applies it. Kourtney took to Poosh.com to discuss her love for the serum and wrote, "It literally took us 12 iterations to get the formula right, so I hope you’re going to be obsessed." 

2 She Uses A Derma Roller

After applying her beloved serum, Kourtney uses her Hora X Poosh: Derma Roller, however she explains she does this step three times a week. She states in her Go to Bed with Me episode, the importance of soaking the derma roller in rubbing alcohol "for at least ten minutes" after using it last and then will rinse it before using it on her face. Sophie Lonsinger writes on Poosh.com, using a derma roller at home "is one of the best ways to maximize the penetration of your products and stimulate the growth of your collagen and skin elasticity." Once she he completes the derma roller process, Kourtney reapplies her serum.

1 She Applies Moisturizer

Kourtney explains in the episode, she often doesn't have to use a moisturizer due to the Hyaluronic acid in her serum. Although, when her skin does feel dry she likes to use the Dr. Barbara Sturm: Face Cream Light and says "my skin can tend to breakout so this one, really, does not make me breakout." She adds a surprising piece of information at the end of the episode and unveils that she does not use eye cream simply because she doesn't like it. She ends off the video with her fresh glowing skin and bible she looks amazing.
