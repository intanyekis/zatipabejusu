Dax Shepard Admits The Hollywood Protests Are Making Him Worried About Money

 Posted on May 28, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  485 words  |  Larita Shotwell

In the midst of the ongoing SAG-AFTRA and WGA strikes that are causing financial instability in the entertainment industry, Dax Shepard's recent remarks about the impact it’s had on his finances have sparked controversy due to his considerable net worth.

In a recent episode of his popular podcast, Armchair Expert, Dax Shepard welcomed Jason Derulo as a guest, and the two delved into discussions about the strain that financial disagreements can inflict on relationships.

Shepard candidly confessed that the financial pressures stemming from the strikes have seeped into his personal life, leading to heated arguments. Expressing his current state, he revealed, "I am currently in a, like, two-month spiral of just completely out of hand financial insecurity."

He admitted to grappling with fears of potential destitution, pondering the demise of his podcasting endeavors and the looming possibility of an actors' strike that might disrupt his acting career.

While acknowledging the root of these anxieties isn't solely linked to the ongoing events, Shepard attributed a significant portion of his unease to his childhood, which was marked by financial instability.

He revealed, "It’s not related to reality. It’s from growing up poor. I just can’t shake it."

This apprehension is particularly poignant for him as a father, fearing a recurrence of the financial struggles he experienced during his childhood.

Contrary to appearances, both Dax Shepard and his wife Kristen Bell have amassed substantial wealth. Shepard's achievements in television, including roles in series like It’s Always Sunny in Philadelphia, King of the Hill, and My Name is Earl, as well as film projects like Idiocracy and CHIPS, have contributed to his reported net worth of $40 million.

Similarly, Kristen Bell, recognized for her prominent role in Veronica Mars and a successful film and television career, boasts an estimated net worth of $40 million.

Nonetheless, the ongoing industry strikes could potentially exert strain on their marital bond. While they've often projected a strong partnership, the couple has been candid about their marital challenges and have even sought counseling to navigate their differences.

Reflecting on the complexities of their relationship, Kristen Bell acknowledged, "I felt very inauthentic to pretend like it’s easy because it’s not, it is so hard." She shared their journey of personal growth, acknowledging the need for a "bigger toolbox" to navigate disagreements and maintain love.

The couple, who has been together since 2007 and officially married in 2013, has weathered various phases of their relationship, fortified by their shared experiences.

With a family that includes two daughters born in 2013 and 2014, Dax Shepard and Kristen Bell continue to grapple with the challenges that come with financial uncertainties, even as they leverage their substantial wealth and their determination to nurture a resilient partnership.
