Career

Kanwal is a Chevening scholar who studied journalism at Delhi University and completed a program in international broadcast journalism at Cardiff University. He was also awarded a Rory Peck Trust grant to attend a course on hostile environment journalism. [number six] He began his career as an anchor-cum-reporter with Zee News in 1999 and then moved on to Aaj Tak in 2002. He has also worked as an Editor-at-Large for the TV Today Group’s Aaj Tak and India Today. Kanwal spoke with India’s union minister Piyush Goyal in 2019 and asked him about the Balakot strikes. In his TV program, he was chastised for simulating a skirmish between the CRPF and Naxal insurgents.
