4 Jared Leto's Music Royalties

As a musician, Jared Leto gets paid royalties when his songs are played on the radio, in public places, or in other media. Performance rights organizations (PROs) like ASCAP and BMI collect these fees, and then the lead singer gets a cut of the money made, thus increasing Jared Leto's net worth.

3 Jared Leto's Movie Deals

Jared Leto has earned varying amounts of money for his roles in big-budget movies over the years.
