Mike Leach Children: Meet Cody, Kim, Kiersten, And Janeen: Mike Leach, officially known as Michael Charles Leach was born to Frank Leach and Sandra Leach in Susanville, California, United States on March 9, 1961

He was raised as a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Mike Leach graduated from Cody High School in 1979. Mike Leach played rugby for the Brigham Young University Men’s Rugby Team from 1979 to 1983 and as a coach, he served for Cal Poly, College of the Desert, Pori Bears, Iowa Wesleyan, Valdosta State, Kentucky, Oklahoma, Texas Tech, and Washington State.
