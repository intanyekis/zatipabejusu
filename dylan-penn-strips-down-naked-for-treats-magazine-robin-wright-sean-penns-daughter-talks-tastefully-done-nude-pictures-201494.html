Dylan Penn Naked on Treats Cover: Sean Penn, Robin Wrights Daughter

 Posted on July 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  353 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Eat your heart out, Playboy! Dylan Penn, the stunning daughter of Sean Penn and Robin Wright, strips down for her first-ever naked cover shoot in the Spring 2014 issue of Treats! magazine, which was revealed Wednesday, April 9.

The budding model, actress and screenwriter, 22, has previously posed for high fashion mags including Elle and W, most recently baring her cleavage and bikini body for GQ‘s January 2014 issue. The blonde Hollywood scion, however, opted to bare all for Treats, which touts itself as a fine art quarterly with “sexual appeal.”

Photographer Tony Duran took the images of Penn, including ones of her covered in a mesh top, wearing nothing but chain mail in another, and provocatively staring down the camera lens in one close-up shot. “I watched, for glimmering moments, the tomboy leave Dylan’s body,” Duran told the mag. “All self doubt, all expected disapproval, all inhibitions. Just for moments. But I was there to capture those moments and document the beginnings of what I am sure will be a powerful woman and beauty icon.”

Penn added that Duran was actually the sole reason why she posed for Treats. “I knew his photography from before I was asked to shoot and really admired him as an artist,” she told the mag. “I have always loved his black and white nudes, they were always so tastefully done and, more specifically, showed that certain elegant yet strong beauty that I think all women embody.”

Penn, who’s become a mega-hot commodity in the last year, actually turned down Playboy before posing nude for Treats, the quarterly shared Wednesday.
