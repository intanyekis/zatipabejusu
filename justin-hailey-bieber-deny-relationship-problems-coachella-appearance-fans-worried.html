Justin And Hailey Bieber Deny Relationship Problems With Cute Coachella Appearance While Fans Remain

Highlights

  • Justin and Hailey Bieber share a sweet moment at Coachella despite ongoing divorce rumors, captured in viral video.
  • Couple seen together watching Lana Del Ray perform, countering rumors of relationship trouble circulating online.
  • Hailey addresses false reports on social media, insisting that the stories about her and Justin are always wrong.

Justin and Hailey Bieber looked close and cute at Coachella this weekend, despite continuing rumors that the couple are headed towards divorce.

During the inaugural night of the California music festival, Justin and Hailey were captured in a heartfelt moment as they watched Lana Del Ray's performance. Someone behind them in the crowd took video of the couple exchanging a moment before Justin kisses his wife’s head, as they enjoy the music, with the clip now going viral on social media.

Before the performance, Justin, 30, was sighted strolling solo, while his counterpart, Hailey, 27 was busy promoting her Rhode skincare line at a booth. However, they appeared to have reunited ahead of Del Rey’s performance to watch it together.

Why Fans Think Something Is Wrong Between Justin And Hailey

Since tying the knot in 2018, the couple has been grappling with persistent doubts regarding their relationship. But the rumors have been further fueled in recent months due to their parent’s online behavior.

In February, Stephen Baldwin, Hailey's father, shared a video from Victor Marx, the founder of All Things Possible Ministries, urging fans to remember the couple in their prayers.

"Christians, please when you think of Justin & Hailey, take a moment to offer a little prayer for them to have wisdom, protection and to draw close to the Lord," conveyed a message over a clip of Justin performing a hym.

The request for prayer immediately left many fans convinced the couple’s relationship was in trouble. Hailey reportedly confronted her dad in private about the post, which has seemingly strained their relationship. She later seemed to indirectly reference it when urging her followers not to believe unconfirmed reports circulating online.

Via her Instagram Story, Hailey asserted, "Just FYI the stories and constant ‘blind items’ I see on TikTok are 100% of the time wrong. Made out of thin air… come from the land of delusion."

She concluded with a firm statement, "So I know it may be fun feeding into these stories but just know they’re always false xx sorry to spoil it."

Reports Claim Hailey Needs A “Break” From Justin

Despite the couple’s insistence that everything is fine, from Hailey’s pleas online to their PDA during recent outings, reports still allege Justin and Hailey are facing a challenge in their relationship. One recent report even claims the model feels as though she needs a break from her musician husband.

“Hailey’s struggling,” a source told In Touch, adding , “She just needs time to sort things out on her own.”

However, the source emphasized Hailey doesn’t’ want to get a divorce, but she may need time apart. “Hailey’s not asking for a divorce,” they added. “She knows Justin feels hurt and confused, but she feels lost too. She just needs a break.”

Justin’s mother seemed to fuel concern about their marriage when some fans were convinced a series of quotes she posted on Instagram were a direct reference to her daughter-in-law. However, with Justina and Hailey staying silent on the reports, it seems fans will have to keep on guessing.
