Craig Ferguson’s Wiki-Bio: Early Life, Education, and Family

Craig Ferguson was born on May 17, 1962, in Springburn, Glasgow, Scotland. His father’s name is Robert Ferguson and mother is Janet Ferguson.

He grew up along with three siblings: two sisters Janice and Lynn Ferguson and an older brother Scott Ferguson. His younger sister, Lynn is a successful writer, actress, and story coach.

When he was six months old, his family shifted to a council house in Cumbernauld. He went to Muirfield Primary School and then in Cumbernauld High School.

At 16, he left Cumbernauld High School to become an electronics technician at a local American company factory Burroughs Corporation.

After that, he worked in construction in Harlem and later became a bouncer at a nightclub in NewYork.
