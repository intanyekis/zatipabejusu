Captain Puffy is an American Twitch streamer, gamer, and YouTuber who is best known for her gaming-based Twitch streaming channel. Her content mostly revolves around the gameplay of the online multiplayer social deduction game Among Us and the sandbox video game series Minecraft. With more than 1.3 million followers on the platform, she is one of the most widely followed female content creators on Twitch. She also runs a similarly themed, self-titled YouTube channel that has amassed more than 200k subscribers. She is popular on other social media platforms as well with more than 950k followers on Twitter and more than 500k followers on Instagram. Captain Puffy had started playing Minecraft in 2010 and had joined the Dream SMP (Survival Multiplayer) team on November 16, 2020. It is a whitelisted team of Minecraft players who play on the Dream Team server, a private multiplayer server in which all the players create an improbable plot, server story, and history. Her reputation and fame received a shot in the arm once she had joined the team. Before this, she used to be a part of Origins, a Minecraft roleplay group. She has also participated in the MC Championship, with Team Cyan Creepers.

Captain Puffy Facts

  • Her screen name/online user name, Captain Puffy, comes from Jigglypuff, her favorite Pokémon character, as she is a big fan of the game.
  • She owns several pets, including 3 cats named Bubble, Peach, and Blue, and a dog named Buttons. She has one other cat and 2 other dogs, all of whom are rescued animals. Captain Puffy also used to have a bearded dragon (a genus of reptiles containing 6 lizard species) named Zippy.
  • She had once taken a colorblindness test during a Twitch stream through which she discovered that she has severe protanopia (color blindness that occurs due to insensitivity to red light, causing confusion between green, red, and yellow). The defect is often hereditary and is one of the most common forms of color blindness. She then started to mainly use a rainbow Minecraft skin because she could see the more saturated colors much clearer.
  • When she had joined the Dream SMP (Survival Multiplayer) team on November 16, 2020, she was just the 3rd female to do so after ItsAlyssa and Nihachu.
  • Captain Puffy is lactose intolerant, suffers from asthma, and also has OCD (Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder). She is allergic to mustard and hates any food containing the ingredient.
  • She is left-handed and has an irrational fear of butterflies.
  • On April 1, 2021, she had tweeted out a fake screenshot claiming that she had been invited to Hermitcraft, and would be quitting Dream SMP.
