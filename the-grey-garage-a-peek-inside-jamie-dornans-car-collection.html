The Irish model and actor is known for his Fifty Shades of Grey role. The drama Belfast (2021) won him critical acclaim and a Golden Globe nomination for Best Supporting Actor. Jamie has garnered phenomenal success over the years, having modeled for top fashion houses and is the face of Dior’s Homme Fragrance line. Like his studded career, he owns a fancy lineup of automobiles like the Audi R8, Jaguar XF, and Audi Q3.

The $198,900 Audi R8 seen in Fifty Shades of Grey is one of the many in his collection.

Audi R8 ($198,900)

Appearing in key scenes of the hit romantic trilogy Fifty Shades of Grey, the Audi R8 is one of the top favorites of the musician-turned-actor. The super sports car comes with a 5.2 liter naturally aspirated V10 engine. Ceramic brakes, virtual cockpit with sport mode and MMI navigation, and 562 horsepower with 406 Lb-ft of torque. An excellent 13-speaker-enabled sound system. The estimated market price of this top-speed beast is $198,900.

Range Rover Vogue ($125,600)

The crafted masterpiece is a great way to travel on the road for long destinations. According to Autoexpress, the spacious SUV comes at an estimated $125,600 with a dynamic glass roof, automatically controlled heated/cool seats, a 360-degree view parking camera, and a WiFi access point. The beast comes powered by a 5.0-liter supercharged V8 engine. Capable of generating 650 NM of torque speed with 450 horsepower. During film promotions, Dornan has been papped on several occasions exiting out of the luxurious sedan with his Fifty Shades of Grey co-actor Dakota Johnson.

BMW 7-Series ($98,700)

Poised to be like a seventh-heaven experience on rough roads, the ultra-stylish BMW 7-series is estimated at a price value of $98,700. The plush interiors come with massage-featured passenger seats, different color displays for entertainment, ambient lighting, and wireless charging. With a 331 Lb-ft torque speed and 335 horsepower, the voguish car is powered by a 3.0 liter twin-turbo six-cylinder base engine. The handsome model has been seen driving the high-profile vehicle to red-carpet appearances.

Cadillac CT6 ($97,490)

According to Forbes, the platinum luxury sedan symbolizes opulence and power. The latest model comes with additional features like an upgraded twin-turbo engine. The safety system includes forward collision alerts and blind spot monitoring to prevent accidents. The 3.0 liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces 400 Lb-ft torque with 404 horsepower. A metallic finished exterior matches the sleek interior. The five-seater sedan offers high performance with automatic transmission.

Mercedes C63 AMG ($78,900)

According to 21 Motoring, the AMG C-class series has the most powerful coupe in the Mercedes brand. The opulent five-seater has automatic climate control, a multi-functioning steering wheel, a wide touch screen for entertainment, and power steering. The 4.0-liter Bi-Turbo V8 engine produces over 500 HP and 700 NM of torque. The Fall actor has been papped driving this deluxe model for events.

Audi RS5 ($75,900)

The four-seater luxe coupe has a mileage of 8.8Kmpl, and its 2.9-liter six-cylinder generates 443.87 bhp, which matches 444 horsepower. The unique feature is the beast engine can achieve zero to sixty miles per hour speed within 3.3 seconds with a torque speed of 155 mph. The safety system includes anti-lock braking, a child-seat mount, six airbags, and a lane watch camera. It has an excellent navigation system, powered steering, powered windows, and automatic cruise control. Jamie has been spotted driving the ultimate $75,900 worth of race machine for errands.

Jaguar XF ($67,900)

The Jaguar XF is the king of the road regarding speed and comfort. It generates 296 horsepower in high torque mode with 246 base horsepower in normal mode. The car can gain 60 mph in 6.2 secs, entering a peak of 129 mph. The smooth ride also features an eight-speed automatic gearbox. A 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine is its technical muscle power. The ride comes at an estimated $67,900 price tag.

Audi Q3 ($43,900)

According to Carwow, the quaint and compact luxury sedan comes at an estimated price range of $43,900. The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine can generate approximately 228 horsepower. The power-packed vehicle can go from 0 to 60 mph in 7.0 secs, peaking at 130 mph. The five-seater has dual-zone electronic climate control, a multi-function steering wheel, manual seat adjustments, and a child seat mount. The safety features include an Audi lane departure warning, rear parking sensors, a smartphone interface, and legal emergency calling.

Jamie Dornan has selected high-performance-based automobiles that come in opulent designs. A fan of speed, most of his favorite cars come equipped with an automatic torque value. His collection is a racer’s paradise.
