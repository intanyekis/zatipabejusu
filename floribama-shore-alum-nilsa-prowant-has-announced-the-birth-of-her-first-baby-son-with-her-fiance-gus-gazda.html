Floribama Shore Alum Nilsa Prowant Has Announced The Birth Of Her First Baby Son With Her Fiance Gus

 Posted on September 5, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  211 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Nilsa Prowant and her fiance, Gus Gazda have welcomed their first child, a son, in the hospital in Atlanta.

Floribama Shore star Nilsa Prowant, 27, is now reportedly the sexiest mon alive. She and her husband to be Gus welcomed son Gray Allen Gazda on Thursday, May 20, 2021, at 1:56 AM. They announced on their respective social media account. 

The new mom has shared series of pictures of herself holding her newborn baby boy along with Gazda on her Instagram account. " 31 hours of labour to get you here and I would do it all over again just for you", she captioned.

The baby weighed 7lbs, 14 oz and measuring 1/2 inches long. "He's totally perfect in every single possible way. They are so blessed!" she wrote. Also, she thanked God for their beautiful baby boy.

In December 2020, the new momma announced her pregnancy news via her Instagram account. Later, she revealed at her 37 weeks pregnancy she had been diagnosed with polyhydramnios.

This is the first child of the couple, who announced their engagement on Nilsa's 27th birthday on January 2 2021. "she can’t believe she get to marry her best friend and bring their son Gray into this world soon", she wrote on her Instagram.
