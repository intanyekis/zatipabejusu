The Kardashian-Jenner Family Surrounded By The Paparazzi At Kylie Cosmetics LA Launch Event

 Posted on May 25, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  453 words  |  Martina Birk

Kylie Jenner, who is the youngest member of the Kardashian-Jenner family and runs her makeup line, brought her daughter Stormi with her to a party at Ulta Beauty in Westwood on Wednesday night. Kylie wore a blousy, white unbuttoned shirt dress with baggy long sleeves and a form-fitting shirt, complemented by white futuristic thick-framed sunglasses and an oversized sequin clutch in the shape of lipstick. Meanwhile, Stormi wore an asymmetrical silver top and matching skirt paired with wrap-around sunglasses and a small triangular-shaped clutch.

The launch of the lip kit—released in 2016 and packaged inside a millennial pink box—was touted as the start of a new era for Kylie Cosmetics. Past editions have included a lip pencil and a matte liquid lipstick.

The Kardashian-Jenner sisters channeled their Barbiecore vibes at the launch party. Khloé, 38, and Kris Jenner, 66, looked hot in pink, while Kim K and Kourtney Kardashian went monochrome with all-black ensembles. The Skims mogul, 41, was sleek in a black halter top, leather pants, and over the knee boots. The Poosh founder, 43, paired a black leather corset top with black pants."

The line was revamped with new packaging that meets clean and vegan standards.

In November 2019, the beauty conglomerate Coty purchased a 51% stake in Jenner's cosmetics and skincare businesses, working closely with the businesswoman (who still maintains creative control of her brands) to revamp Kylie Cosmetics.

During the relaunch, Jenner also revealed that her self-made empire is for her daughter if she wants to take over someday.

In a special edition of tmrw magazine, Jenner said: "Stormi is my legacy. I'm raising her to be smart and kind and driven. Maybe she will take over Kylie Cosmetics one day? Only if she wants to."

When she gave her followers a look at the creation process of her popular cosmetics brand through a series of photos taken at what appeared to be a manufacturing lab in Milan, Italy, the influencer faced backlash from some members of the public.

Kylie Jenner was quickly questioned by her followers about the photos, wondering why she wasn't wearing personal protective equipment like gloves, a mask, and a hair net.

The self-made billionaire, who began her business at a young age and has since become a billionaire, explained that she takes pictures in a small personal space where she creates "my own fun samples and takes pictures for content." The space is located "nowhere near the mass manufacturing."
