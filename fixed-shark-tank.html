What is Fixed? Who is the founder of Fixed?

Fixed is a mobile application designed to assist users in challenging potential parking fines. The app provides a convenient solution for individuals who want to dispute their parking tickets.

Users can easily upload a photo of their parking ticket through the Fixed app. The app then utilizes advanced scanning technology to detect any errors or irregularities on the ticket. This feature allows users to identify potential grounds for contesting the fine.

After analyzing the uploaded ticket, Fixed generates customized letters that users can use to formally dispute the ticket’s issuance. These letters are tailored to reflect the specific details of each case and help users present a strong argument to contest the ticket.

Fixed is a game-changer for people dealing with unfair parking fines. With its user-friendly interface and automated letter generation, it takes the hassle out of challenging tickets and increases the chances of a successful appeal.” – Parking Solutions Magazine

The founder of Fixed is David Hegarty. He is a California native who holds an engineering degree from University College Dublin and an MBA from Northwestern University. Hegarty created Fixed to address the difficulties and inefficiencies that people face when contesting parking tickets.

With his background in engineering and business, Hegarty combined his expertise to develop Fixed as a user-friendly and effective solution for challenging parking fines.

The success of Fixed can be attributed to its innovative approach and the dedication of its founder to simplifying the process of contesting parking tickets.

Fixed Shark Tank Pitch

During his appearance on Shark Tank, David Hegarty pitched Fixed, the parking ticket app, in the hopes of securing a $700,000 investment for a 5% equity stake in the company. The pitch revolved around the innovative solution that Fixed offered for challenging unfair parking fines. With the app, users could simply upload a photo of their ticket, which would then be scanned for errors. The app would generate customized dispute letters, simplifying the process and increasing the chances of getting the ticket dismissed.

“Fixed is not only a convenient tool for individuals, but it also has the potential to disrupt and improve the parking ticket industry as a whole.”

In exchange for the investment, Fixed was valued at $14 million at the time of the pitch. Although Mark Cuban expressed interest and offered the desired investment amount, a deal was never finalized. However, the exposure on Shark Tank proved to be a turning point for Fixed, helping the company raise over $1.8 million in funding from other sources.
